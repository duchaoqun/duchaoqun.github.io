第8章 - 骄傲的玫瑰花

The shrub soon stopped growing, and began to get ready to produce a flower. The little prince, who was present at the first appearance of a huge bud, felt at once that some sort of miraculous apparition must emerge from it.

灌木很快停止了生长，开始准备开花。小王子目睹了一个巨大花苞的初次出现，立刻感觉到某种神奇的东西即将从中诞生。

But the flower was not satisfied to complete the preparations for her beauty in the shelter of her green chamber. She chose her colours with the greatest care. She adjusted her petals one by one. She did not wish to go out into the world all rumpled, like the field poppies. It was only in the full radiance of her beauty that she wished to appear. Oh, yes! She was a coquettish creature! And her mysterious adornment lasted for days and days.

但这朵花并不满足于在绿色的花房里完成她的美丽准备。她非常仔细地挑选着自己的颜色。她一片一片地调整着花瓣。她不希望像田野里的罂粟花那样皱巴巴地出现在世界上。她只希望在自己的美丽绽放得最充分的时候出现。哦，是的！她是一个爱撒娇的家伙！她神秘的装扮持续了好几天。

I soon learned to know this flower better. On the little prince's planet the flowers had always been very simple. They had only one ring of petals; they took up no room at all; they were a trouble to nobody. One morning they would appear in the grass, and by night they would have faded peacefully away.

我很快就更了解这朵花了。在小王子的星球上，花一直都很简单。它们只有一圈花瓣；它们完全不占地方；它们不给任何人添麻烦。早上它们会出现在草地上，晚上就会悄然凋零。

But one day, from a seed blown from no one knew where, a new flower had come up; and the little prince had watched very closely over this small sprout which was not like any other small sprouts on his planet. It might, you see, have been a new kind of baobab.

但是有一天，不知从哪里吹来的一粒种子，长出了一朵新的花；小王子密切关注着这个小嫩芽，它与他星球上的其他小嫩芽都不一样。你看，它可能是一种新型的猴面包树。

Then one morning, exactly at sunrise, she suddenly showed herself.

然后，在一个清晨，正好是日出时分，她突然绽放了。

"Oh! How beautiful you are!"

"哦！你真美！"

"Ah! I am scarcely awake. I beg that you will excuse me. My petals are still all disarranged..."

"啊！我还没完全醒来。请原谅我。我的花瓣还乱蓬蓬的……"

The little prince could guess easily enough that she was not any too modest -- but how moving -- and exciting -- she was!

小王子很容易就猜到她并不太谦虚——但她是多么动人——多么令人心驰神往啊！

And, after working with all this painstaking precision, she yawned and said:

在如此精心地准备之后，她打了个哈欠，说道：

"I think it is time for breakfast," she added an instant later. "If you would have the kindness to think of my needs --"

"我想该吃早餐了，"她过了一会儿又补充道。"如果你能好心考虑一下我的需要——"

So the little prince, completely abashed, went to look for a sprinkling-can of fresh water. So, he tended the flower.

于是小王子感到非常尴尬，去寻找一个装着清水的喷壶。就这样，他开始照料这朵花。

So, too, she began very quickly to torment him with her vanity -- which was, if the truth be known, a little difficult to deal with. One day, for instance, when she was speaking of her four thorns, she said to the little prince:

同样，她很快就开始用她的虚荣心折磨他——说实话，这有点难以应对。例如，有一天，当她谈到她的四根刺时，她对小王子说：

"Let the tigers come with their claws!"

"让老虎带着它们的爪子来吧！"

"There are no tigers on my planet," the little prince objected. "And, anyway, tigers do not eat weeds."

"我的星球上没有老虎，"小王子反驳道。"而且，老虎也不吃草。"

"I am not a weed," the flower replied, sweetly.

"我不是草，"花甜甜地回答。

"Please excuse me..."

"请原谅我……"

"I am not at all afraid of tigers," she went on, "but I have a horror of drafts. I suppose you wouldn't have a screen for me?"

"我一点也不怕老虎，"她继续说道，"但我很怕风。我想你不会有屏风给我吧？"

"A horror of drafts -- that is bad luck, for a plant," remarked the little prince, and added to himself, "This flower is a very complex creature..."

"怕风——对于植物来说，这可真是不幸，"小王子说道，心里想，"这朵花真是个复杂的生物……"

"At night I want you to put me under a glass globe. It is very cold where you live. In the place I came from --"

"晚上我希望你把我放在玻璃罩下。你住的地方很冷。在我来的地方——"

But she interrupted herself at that point. She had come in the form of a seed. She could not have known anything of any other worlds. Embarrassed over having let herself be caught on the verge of such a native untruth, she coughed two or three times, in order to put the little prince in the wrong.

但她在这一点上打断了自己。她是以种子的形式来的。她不可能知道任何其他世界的事情。她为自己在这样一个明显的谎言边缘被抓住而感到尴尬，于是咳嗽了两三次，想让小王子处于错误的位置。

"The screen?"

"屏风？"

"I was just going to look for it when you spoke to me..."

"你跟我说话的时候，我正要去找它……"

Then she forced her cough a little more so that he should suffer from remorse just the same.

然后她更用力地咳嗽了几声，好让他也感到内疚。

So the little prince, in spite of all the good will that was inseparable from his love, had soon come to doubt her. He had taken seriously words which were without importance, and it made him very unhappy.

因此，尽管小王子的爱中充满了善意，但他很快就开始怀疑她了。他把那些无关紧要的话当真了，这让他非常不开心。

And he continued his confidences:

他继续倾诉：

"I ought not to have listened to her," he confided to me one day. "One never ought to listen to the flowers. One should simply look at them and breathe their fragrance. Mine perfumed all my planet. But I did not know how to take pleasure in all her grace. This tale of claws, which disturbed me so much, should only have filled my heart with tenderness and pity."

"我不应该听她的，"有一天他向我吐露心声。"一个人永远不应该听花的话。一个人应该只是看看它们，呼吸它们的芬芳。我的花让我的整个星球都充满了香气。但我不知道如何欣赏她所有的优雅。那个爪子的故事，让我如此不安，本应该只让我的心中充满温柔和怜悯。"

"The fact is that I did not know how to understand anything! I ought to have judged by deeds and not by words. She cast her fragrance and her radiance over me. I ought never to have run away from her... I ought to have guessed all the affection that lay behind her poor little stratagems. Flowers are so inconsistent! But I was too young to know how to love her..."

"事实上，我根本不知道如何理解任何事情！我应该根据行动而不是言语来判断。她用她的芬芳和光芒笼罩着我。我永远不应该离开她……我应该猜出她那些可怜的小把戏背后隐藏的所有感情。花是如此的善变！但我太年轻了，不知道如何爱她……"
